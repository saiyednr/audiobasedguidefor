CULTURE OF GUJARAT !

The Challenge

The World Heritage Sites in India, despite their scale and grandeur, lack authentic interpretation in vernacular languages for the myriad audiences visiting them.

 

The local artisans in and around these sites are seldom compensated fairly for their products and services leading to many of them drifting away from their traditional crafts to more mass-produced goods.

 

The heritage sites have become mere picnic spots and Instagram opportunity for children visiting them, instead of being knowledge dissemination centres.

The Initiative

Traveler's Bible offers a platform that integrates new-age technology of Augmented Reality (AR), Geo Fencing and Multimedia to enhance visitor experience of the World Heritage Sites in India and connect with host communities.

 

The content for these sites is acurated with support from the globally renowned knowledge partners in the cultural heritage and conservation domains.

The Impact

Traveler's Bible self-guided heritage trails help travelers experience the culture, art, and culinary delights of the local communities and encourage them to buy authentic products directly from the artisans, thereby creating a viable livelihood for them. Through its school outreach program, Traveler Bible enables children apply classroom knowledge using the Gujarat Heritage Sites as premises for knowledge dissemination.

 

Traveler Bible, in alignment with United Nations' Sustainable Development Goals 4, 8, and 11, promotes and preserves intangible cultural heritage of India, enables community employability, and facilitates experiential education for sustainable development.
